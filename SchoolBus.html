"Specially featured and designed Track my wheels school bus tracking system that ensures student`s safety and well managed student transportation. It also comprises student`s attendance system by means of RFID technology which is one of the most efficient and accurate attendance systems. Parents can track location, status of their child from anywhere by logging into web based/ mobile applications."

School Bus Track Key Features

  • Student Ridership Tracking.
  • Send SMS & Email alert to parent`s mobile & email account 5 minutes before the bus reaching the pickup & drop spot.
  • Parents can track online location of the bus from their office, home or from any other remote place to know if everything is all right.
  • RFID based attendance database. Student records are stored at server database. This is ensures child safety and error free tracking of his/her attendance.
  • Secured access via user ID and Password on portal gives you view of the entire workforce on one map.
  • Route Replays / Route followed / Route deviation readily available on map allows you to analyze, track and opt imize your workforce.
  • Receive alerts via email, SMS and push notifications on iOS® and Android® devices.
  • MIS reports including Travel History, Task report, Tracking Report, Stoppage Report, Timely Arrival/Departure Report ,Deviation report deliver an snapshot for you to make quicker business decisions
